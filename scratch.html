Market Forecasting & Analysis

My interest with global market forecasting came about in one of my first finance classes here at BYU. What drew me in was how data-driven predictions can point out future market trends and help businesses make strategic decisions. Through my research and coursework, I've developed a deep interest and motivation to understand the relationships between economic indicators, market behavior, and predictive analytics.
